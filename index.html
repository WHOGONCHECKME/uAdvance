Why uAdvance

Tailored to financial services in Australia

We train financial services individuals and teams in Australia. The aim is simple. You build the technical skills and keep the domain knowledge that makes those skills useful at work.

Most analytics courses teach the tools and little else. Their worked examples aren't financial services industry specific and usually centre around retail or marketing. As a result you are left to translate everything back to your own work. We start from the work. Every example is focused on real situations in financial services.

We teach the way people learn on the job. Each session builds toward a realistic workplace task and you apply the tools and skills as they are introduced.

Because the content is specific to the industry, you may be able to claim CPD hours. Check the requirements with your relevant professional body.

What you or your team gets

  • Industry specific content
  • Hands on practical work with minimal theory
  • CPD hours may be available
  • Certificate on completion & wording for LinkedIn
  • Often covered by an employer professional development budget
