Why uAdvance

Tailored to financial services in Australia

We train financial services teams in Australia. The aim is simple. Your staff build the technical skills and keep the domain knowledge that makes those skills useful at work.

Most analytics courses teach the tool and little else. The worked examples come from retail or marketing, and your team is left to translate everything back to its own work. We start from the work instead. Every example is drawn from banking, insurance, super or wealth.

We teach the way people learn on the job. Each session builds toward a realistic workplace task, and your team applies the tools and skills as they are introduced.

Because the content is specific to the industry, your team may be able to claim CPD hours. Check the requirements with your relevant professional body.

What your team gets

  • Industry-specific content
  • Hands-on practical work with minimal theory
  • CPD hours may be available
  • Certificate on completion
  • Often covered by an employer professional development budget
